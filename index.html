About Me

Designing with Purpose, Centered on Learners

I'm a PhD student in Instructional Design and Technology with a background in data analysis, online instruction, and student support. I draw on learning theory and research to design learning experiences that are effective, approachable, and thoughtfully planned, from course structure to assessment to interaction.

I've partnered with faculty, academic teams, and institutional leaders to strengthen student outcomes through course design, analytics, and collaborative problem-solving. Whatever the project, my aim is to make learning clearer, more engaging, and more meaningful for the people it serves.

I live in Idaho with my husband and our two teenage sons. When I'm not working or schooling, you can usually find me traveling, trying new foods, or surrounded by books—collecting them, reading them, and always looking for the next one to add to my shelf.
